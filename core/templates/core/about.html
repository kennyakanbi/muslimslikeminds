About Muslims Like Minds

A community of believers united by faith, knowledge, and service to humanity

Who We Are

Muslims Like Minds is a faith-driven community dedicated to nurturing spiritual growth, authentic Islamic knowledge, and positive character among Muslims.

We bring together believers of shared values and vision to learn, grow, and contribute meaningfully to society through teachings of Islam, media engagement, and service to humanity.

Our mission is to embody the beauty of Islam through action, wisdom, and compassion in today’s modern world.

Our Mission

To nurture faith, spread authentic Islamic knowledge, and inspire positive character through teaching, media, and community service.

Our Vision

To build a united Muslim community that reflects the beauty of Islam and positively impacts society at large.

Our Values

Faith, sincerity, compassion, integrity, unity, and service to humanity.

What We Do

Our activities are rooted in knowledge, media, and community service

Islamic Teachings

We provide authentic Islamic teachings through lectures, classes, and educational content aimed at strengthening faith and understanding.

Media & Outreach

Leveraging media to share beneficial knowledge, inspire positive change, and amplify meaningful Islamic narratives.

Community Service

We engage in humanitarian efforts and community projects that reflect Islam’s emphasis on compassion, charity, and service.
